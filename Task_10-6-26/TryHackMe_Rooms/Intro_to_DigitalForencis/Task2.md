# Task 2: Digital Forensics Process

Digital forensics follows a structured process to preserve the integrity of digital evidence.

## Process

1. **Acquire the evidence** from the crime scene.
2. **Establish a Chain of Custody** to track who handles the evidence.
3. **Secure and transport** the evidence to the forensic lab.
4. **Create a forensic copy** and preserve the original evidence.
5. **Analyze the copy** using forensic tools.
6. **Document and report** the findings.

## Key Principles

* Proper legal authorization
* Chain of Custody
* Data integrity verification using hash functions
* Use of validated tools
* Repeatability of results
* Reporting
